Marketing Analytics

In the "Marketing Analytics" subject, I explored the essential techniques and methodologies used to gather and analyze data for marketing research.
The subject covered survey research, sampling methods, questionnaire design, validation tests, multiple regression analysis, and dimension reduction techniques to gain valuable insights for marketing decision-making.






Assignment

Survey Research

I learned about the fundamentals of survey research, understanding how to design effective surveys to collect relevant and reliable data from respondents. This involved selecting appropriate survey methods and ensuring the validity and reliability of the collected data.


Sampling Methods

I explored various sampling techniques used in marketing research, including random sampling, stratified sampling, and cluster sampling. Understanding these methods allowed me to select representative samples for analysis.


Questionnaire Design

I delved into the art of designing questionnaires that yield valuable insights from survey respondents. I learned how to frame questions, choose response scales, and structure the questionnaire for effective data collection.


Validation Test

I studied validation tests to ensure the accuracy and relevance of collected data. This included techniques like content validity, construct validity, and criterion validity.


Multiple Regression Analysis

I learned about multiple regression analysis, a powerful statistical technique used to explore the relationships between multiple independent variables and a dependent variable. This allowed me to identify significant predictors of consumer behavior or market outcomes.


Dimension Reduction Techniques

I explored dimension reduction techniques such as factor analysis and principal component analysis (PCA). These techniques helped in simplifying complex data structures and identifying underlying patterns in the data.


Outcomes

- Developed a comprehensive understanding of survey research methodologies, ensuring data collection accuracy and relevance.

- Gained proficiency in various sampling methods to select representative samples for analysis.

- Acquired skills in questionnaire design to obtain valuable insights from survey respondents.

- Mastered validation tests to validate the collected data and ensure data quality.

- Explored advanced statistical techniques like multiple regression analysis and dimension reduction for deeper insights into marketing data.
