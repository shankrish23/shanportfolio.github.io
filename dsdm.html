Fundamentals of Data Science and Decision Making

In the "Fundamentals of Data Science and Decision Making" subject, I acquired essential knowledge and skills in data analysis, hypothesis testing, regression analysis, and visualization.
Through practical assignments, I explored the applications of advanced Excel, JMP, and SPSS tools to derive meaningful insights and support informed decision-making.









Assignment

Advanced Excel Data Analysis

I delved into the capabilities of Advanced Excel for data analysis, learning various functions and techniques to manipulate, clean, and organize data. Through practical exercises, I honed my proficiency in handling complex datasets and extracting valuable information.


Hypothesis Testing and t-test

In this assignment, I gained a comprehensive understanding of hypothesis testing principles and techniques. I performed t-tests to compare means and evaluate hypotheses, enabling me to make data-driven decisions with confidence.


Chi-Square Test

I explored the chi-square test to analyze categorical data and assess the relationships between variables. Understanding this statistical test enhanced my ability to draw insights from non-numerical data.


Regression Analysis

I studied regression analysis as a powerful tool for modeling relationships between variables. Through practical exercises, I learned to perform regression analyses, predict outcomes, and interpret regression coefficients.

ANOVA (Analysis of Variance)

I explored ANOVA as a statistical technique to compare means across multiple groups. By conducting ANOVA tests, I gained insights into the variations between different groups and their impact on the dependent variable.


JMP and SPSS Tools

As part of the subject, I learned to use JMP and SPSS, two prominent data analysis software. These tools equipped me to perform advanced statistical analyses, visualize data effectively, and generate valuable insights from complex datasets.


Outcomes

- Developed proficiency in Advanced Excel data analysis, enabling me to handle and analyze diverse datasets efficiently.

- Acquired expertise in hypothesis testing, t-tests, and chi-square tests, allowing me to draw valid conclusions from sample data and categorical variables.

- Mastered regression analysis and ANOVA, empowering me to model relationships and make predictions based on data trends.

- Gained proficiency in using JMP and SPSS software for advanced statistical analysis and data visualization.
